Project Overview

Backpack Wars is essentially a game designed to teach students how to select appropriate marketing channels and the attention to detail needed to succeed in the modern, competitive, economy. It's more or less meant as an easy introduction to the concepts and the end goal is to include hoverable tooltips a graphical GUI and save/load plus scenario selection. For now it's a WiP demo of the concept.

Pedagogical Goals

Connecting to Marketing Concepts

Students can learn to intuit the connection between target markets and advertising channels.

Intended Learning Outcomes

  • How to implement marketing campaigns for diverse products targeting specific niche markets
  • Profit maximization within a given budget through rational allocation of marketing resources
  • Understanding of market capitalization and segmentation
